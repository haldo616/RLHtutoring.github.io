About

RLH Tutoring was set up in January 2023 with the aim of providing a personalised, relaxed approach to learning maths whether that be online or in person. Current exam boards followed include AQA, Edexcel and OCR for GSCE and A- level, whilst iGCSE, the IB and Functional skills can also be catered for where requested as well as more general maths support. By pinpointing topics to be worked on, giving clear explanations and encouraging all the way, children gain confidence and see improved results from one assessment to the next. It is a pleasure to be part of their journey and see them blossom. Rachel
